Short version: DrinkUp does not collect any personal data. There are no user accounts. We use anonymous, aggregated usage analytics (PostHog, EU-hosted) to improve the app — you can opt out at any time in iOS settings.

1. Who we are

This privacy policy applies to the mobile application "DrinkUp" (the "App") for iOS and Android.

Operator (Data Controller):
Michael Haller
Email: michael.haller@unibz.it

2. What data we collect

2.1 Data you provide

The App does not require an account, login, or registration. Player names you enter when setting up a game are stored only locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

2.2 Settings stored locally

The following preferences are stored on your device using the operating system's local storage (AsyncStorage). They never leave your device:

  • Selected language
  • Theme preference (dark/light)
  • Sound and haptic settings
  • Onboarding completion status
  • Player names from previous sessions

2.3 Anonymous analytics

We use PostHog (EU cloud, hosted in Germany) to collect anonymous usage statistics. This helps us understand which games are popular and improve the App. The following anonymous events may be collected:

  • App opened / closed
  • Which game was selected
  • Game settings (e.g. number of players, language, mode)
  • Device type and OS version
  • App version

What we do NOT collect: No name, email, phone number, exact location, IP address (anonymized by PostHog), advertising ID, or any data that could identify you personally.

3. Legal basis (GDPR Art. 6)

  • Local data: Necessary for app functionality (Art. 6(1)(b))
  • Anonymous analytics: Legitimate interest in improving the app (Art. 6(1)(f))

4. Third-party services

4.1 PostHog

Provider: PostHog Inc., hosted on PostHog EU Cloud (Germany). PostHog only receives anonymous events as listed above. PostHog Privacy Policy

4.2 Apple App Store / Google Play

When you download the App, Apple or Google collects information per their own privacy policies. We have no control over and no access to that data beyond aggregated download statistics.

  • Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

5. Data retention

Local data on your device is retained until you delete the App. Anonymous analytics events are retained by PostHog for up to 12 months and then automatically deleted.

6. Your rights (GDPR)

Since we do not collect personal data, most GDPR rights are automatically fulfilled. You still have the right to:

  • Information about data processing (this document)
  • Disable analytics by enabling "Limit Ad Tracking" in iOS settings
  • Delete all local data by uninstalling the App
  • File a complaint with your local data protection authority

7. Children

The App is rated 17+ and intended for adults due to its drinking-game theme. We do not knowingly collect any data from children under 17.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the latest version. Material changes will be communicated through the App.

9. Contact

2. Welche Daten wir erfassen

2.1 Daten, die du angibst

Die App benötigt keinen Account, kein Login und keine Registrierung. Spielernamen, die du beim Einrichten eines Spiels eingibst, werden nur lokal auf deinem Gerät gespeichert und niemals an unsere Server übertragen.

2.2 Lokal gespeicherte Einstellungen

Die folgenden Einstellungen werden auf deinem Gerät über den lokalen Speicher des Betriebssystems (AsyncStorage) gesichert. Sie verlassen dein Gerät nie:

  • Ausgewählte Sprache
  • Theme-Präferenz (Dunkel/Hell)
  • Sound- und Haptik-Einstellungen
  • Onboarding-Status
  • Spielernamen aus vorherigen Sessions

2.3 Anonyme Statistiken

Wir nutzen PostHog (EU-Cloud, gehostet in Deutschland) um anonyme Nutzungsstatistiken zu erfassen. Das hilft uns zu verstehen, welche Spiele beliebt sind und die App zu verbessern. Folgende anonyme Events können erfasst werden:

  • App geöffnet / geschlossen
  • Welches Spiel ausgewählt wurde
  • Spieleinstellungen (z.B. Anzahl Spieler, Sprache, Modus)
  • Gerätetyp und Betriebssystem-Version
  • App-Version

Was wir NICHT erfassen: Keinen Namen, keine E-Mail, keine Telefonnummer, keinen genauen Standort, keine IP-Adresse (von PostHog anonymisiert), keine Werbe-ID und keine Daten, die dich persönlich identifizieren könnten.

3. Rechtsgrundlage (DSGVO Art. 6)

  • Lokale Daten: Erforderlich für die App-Funktionalität (Art. 6 Abs. 1 lit. b)
  • Anonyme Statistiken: Berechtigtes Interesse zur Verbesserung der App (Art. 6 Abs. 1 lit. f)
